Who I Am

I am a passionate WordPress Website Developer and Graphics Designer based in Lagos, Nigeria, with over 4 years of hands-on experience designing, developing, and optimising websites for a wide variety of clients.

Currently serving as a Website Developer and IT Support Officer at the Institute for Industrial Technology (IIT) under the NYSC program.
